About Me

My name is Matthew Backos, and I was born and raised in Johannesburg, South Africa. I am an aspiring web developer and software engineer with a diverse background, having worked as an assistant adventure guide and a professional chef.

I have always been driven by curiosity and a passion for learning, regardless of the nature of the work or the project at hand. This curiosity has led me to develop a deep interest in IT concepts and a love for problem-solving. My goal is to leverage software development to improve my life and the lives of those around me.

I am committed to continuously honing my existing skills while eagerly acquiring new ones that best suit the projects I undertake. I am goal-driven, objective-oriented, and thrive under deadlines. I am excited about contributing to projects and, most importantly, collaborating effectively within a team to achieve success.
