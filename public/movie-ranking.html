The Best Movies According to Matthew

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar - Christpher Nolan

2014

I can safely say this movie will always rank quite high for me. I've always been a huge Christopher Nolan fan and this film ignited a lifelong admiration for cinema.

No country for old men - Joel & Ethan Coen

2007

One of the highlights i can mention from this film is how unsettling it can be at times. It strikes a calculated balance between suspense and action. Combined with the magnificent cast and script it is one for the ages.

Babylon - Damien Chazelle

2022

I think this film was seriously ambitious. Every shot, line of dialogue and decison made while putting this film together was expertly crafted and that sense of care was evident to me. It is a generational masterpiece and all-in-all a benchamrk for beautiful film for years to come.
